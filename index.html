Sofia Bonati

is an Argentinian artist and illustrator. She was born in Buenos Aires on 1982 into a family of artists. She started her artistic career when she moved to the UK in 2013, whilst balancing her new life as a mum.

About Artist

Born into a family of Argentinian artists, illustrator Sofia Bonati studied geology before taking up graphic design and illustration, which she now practices part-time from the UK, her current home. Adding inks, gouache and watercolour to her delicate pencil drawings, Sofia creates portraits of enchanting women with a look of mystery in their eyes.
